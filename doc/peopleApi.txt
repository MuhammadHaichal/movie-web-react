BASE URL PEOPLE LIST
====================

Get a list of people ordered by popularity.

base url = https://api.themoviedb.org/3/person/popular?language=en-US&page=1




DETAIL PEOPLE LIST
==================

Query the top level details of a person


base url = https://api.themoviedb.org/3/person/person_id?language=en-US


SEARCH PEOPLE
=============
Search for people by their name and also known as names.

https://api.themoviedb.org/3/search/person?query=haikal&include_adult=false&language=en-US
